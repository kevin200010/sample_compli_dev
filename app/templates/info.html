What you can do with Eagle Insights

Vulnerability Remediation

When you click the "Vulnerability Remediation" button, you will be redirected to a page displaying all of your critical Security Hub findings. Each finding is detailed, and you can open them up individually to review specifics.

Our system integrates with ChatGPT to provide multiple remediation steps tailored to each finding. Additionally, you have the option to execute the recommended AWS CLI commands directly from this interface, streamlining the remediation process and helping to reduce vulnerabilities in your AWS account.

PCI Analysis

The "PCI Analysis" button initiates a compliance analysis of your AWS services, focusing on PCI DSS. The analysis identifies areas of non-compliance, with results compiled into a detailed PDF report that includes recommendations to achieve full compliance, ensuring customer data protection.

Hipaa Analysis

Clicking the "Hipaa Analysis" button triggers an examination of your AWS services against HIPAA regulations. The results, compiled into a PDF report, will highlight areas of non-compliance and provide actionable recommendations to ensure your AWS environment is HIPAA-compliant.
